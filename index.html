Hello, I'm Ananya.

Hello, I'm Ananya Agarwal, an architect passionate about creating spaces that are meaningful, functional, and rooted in context. My work explores the intersection of architecture, urban design, and culture, with a focus on designing environments that enhance everyday experiences. I believe good architecture goes beyond aesthetics—it responds to people, place, and purpose.

This portfolio showcases a selection of academic and design projects that reflect my approach to thoughtful problem-solving, attention to detail, and continuous learning.
