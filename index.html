Hello, I'm Ananya.

I'm an architecture and design student with a passion for spaces that feel both intentional and alive. My work sits at the intersection of vernacular tradition and contemporary thinking — drawing from materials, light, and the lives of the people who inhabit a place.

Currently pursuing my studies, I use drawing, model-making, and writing as ways of thinking through space before it is built.
